Features of HTML5:
********************

	1) Clean seperation between Markup and Styles
		HTML (vs) CSS

	2) Better support for Search Engines (like Google)

	3) Audio and Video support
		<audio> and <video>

	4) Storage
		Cookies (vs) Local storage and Session storage

	5) Game Development

	6) Mobile
		'Responsive Layout' and 'HTML 5 New Input types'

	7) It's the future
